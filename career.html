VSPTech IT Services Benefits

VSPTech IT Services offers attractive benefit packages to meet the personal needs of our employees and their families. We prefer to be an employer of choice, and the meticulous efforts of our Human Resources ensure that our benefit plans are competitive and comprehensive. VSPTech IT Services believes in holistic approach of covering all needs of employees like health coverage, tuition reimbursement, and green card assistance.


The available benefits are:


  • Group health insurance (PPO/PMO) for the employee and qualified dependents
  • Group Dental & Vision insurance
  • Paid vacation
  • Sick time
  • 401(k) pension plan
  • Professional development
  • Tuition reimbursement
  • Relocation expenses
  • Marginal federal income tax savings
  • Social Security tax savings on out-of-pocket healthcare and dependent care expenses
  • Green card sponsorship in the United States (permanent residents)
